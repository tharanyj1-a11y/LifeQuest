Welcome to LifeQuest

LifeQuest is a gamified web application designed to help users improve essential life skills such as budgeting, wellness, and productivity. By completing daily activities, users can track progress and unlock achievements.

How LifeQuest Works

LifeQuest breaks personal development into interactive modules. Each module focuses on a key area of growth and rewards consistent effort.

  • Track income and expenses in the Money Manager
  • Build healthier routines using the Habits Tracker
  • Increase focus and efficiency in the Productivity Arena
  • Earn badges and rewards through the Achievement System

Get Started

Navigate through the dashboard to begin your journey. LifeQuest is designed to make personal growth simple, motivating, and rewarding.
