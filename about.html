About LifeQuest

LifeQuest is a gamified self-development platform that helps users improve their habits, productivity, finances, and mental wellness through engaging modules, visual progress tracking, and reward-based motivation.

Core Features

🥗 Healthy Habits Tracker

Track meals, sleep, hydration, and workouts. Build streaks and unlock badges.

📓 Personal Journal

Reflect daily, monitor moods, and develop mindfulness routines.

💰 Money Manager

Manage income, expenses, and savings goals with visual budget indicators.

🏆 Achievement System

Earn rewards and badges for completing challenges and maintaining consistency.

⏱ Productivity Arena

Use timers, track tasks, and gain points for focused work sessions.
