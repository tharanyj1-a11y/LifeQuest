About LifeQuest

LifeQuest is a gamified platform focused on helping users improve their habits, wellness, productivity, and self-awareness. Users can track their progress, earn achievements, and build positive routines through engaging modules.

Core Modules

  • Healthy Habits Tracker

    Log daily habits such as meals, water intake, sleep, and exercise. Track wellness metrics and earn badges for consistency.

  • Personal Journal

    Reflect on your day, track moods, and write thoughts. Mindfulness tracking helps improve emotional awareness.

  • Money Manager

    Track income, expenses, and savings goals. Visual feedback helps users understand budgeting progress.

  • Achievement System

    Earn rewards and unlock badges for completing goals and maintaining streaks.

  • Productivity Arena

    Set tasks, use Pomodoro timers, and gain points for focus sessions. Helps users improve time management skills.
